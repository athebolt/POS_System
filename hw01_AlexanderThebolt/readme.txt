POS - Point Of Sale system for AlexMart

Use
Add (or subtract) 6 buyable items on this POS system.

The item in your cart, the quantity of each item, and the subtotal of the item to be purchased
will be displayed along with the subtotal of all item totals combined, the michigan sales tax,
and the total (subtotal + tax).

A New Order button is available below the total to reset the order with an updated time and order number.
